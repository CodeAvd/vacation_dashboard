## Core Design Pillars

```
1. VACATION VIBE        — Every moment feels like rest, not stress
2. MEANINGFUL GUESTS    — Players remember faces, not anonymous tables
3. YOUR CAFE, YOUR STORY — Customization that matters to gameplay
4. PROGRESSION WITH PURPOSE — Never grinding, always discovering
```

## Why These Features?

All features in this GDD are derived from two sources of truth:

1. **Competitive Analysis:** What makes Dave the Diver, Mystia's Izakaya, and Espresso Tycoon achieve 95%+ Steam ratings
2. **Player Voice:** Real feedback from 300+ Steam forum posts and 16 Discord bug/suggestion threads (Feb–Mar 2026)

The goal is not to copy competitors — it's to take the *best mechanic from each* and deliver it at a higher quality than any single competitor has achieved.
